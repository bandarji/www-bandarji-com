Howdy! You have reached the end of the Internet. Welcome to my personal website. I'm a computer geek, working in Silicon Valley for DreamWorks Animation. Last year, my wife and I moved out to Half Moon Bay, just down the coast from San Francisco; no better place to ride a bicycle. I generate a lot of noise with my guitars. Even though I have not finished butchering English, I'm learning a little Hindi.

By day, I do my best to ensure service uptime for the NOVA Technologies division of DreamWorks Animation. My team uses Linux atop OpenStack to deploy containers with OpenShift or Marathon. Send me a note if you have software release experience in a highly-virtualized environment, and would like information on open positions.

Aside from work, I enjoy traveling. I moved to India for work a few years ago. Living overseas for a year, I caught the travel bug. It's hard to believe that I had not left North America until 2008. Since then, in addition to twelve Indian states, I've visited China, Egypt, England, France, Italy and South Africa. Where to next?
